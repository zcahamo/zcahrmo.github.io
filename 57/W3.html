Question 1. (The 1b thing) The number of aces out of 4 cards dealt from a standard pack. This is a hypergeometric distribution. Crucially, these cards are sampled without replacement, so a binomial distribution cannot be used.

\[ X\sim HB(n=4,M=4,N=52)\quad \text {for}\quad k=0,1,2,3,4 \]

So

\[ P(X=k)=\frac {\binom {M}k\binom {N-M}{n-k}}{\binom {N}k} =\frac {\binom 4k\binom {48}{4-k}}{\binom {52}4} \]
